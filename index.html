About Me
"I have no special talent, I am only passionately curious"
- Albert Einstein
In the 15 years of life I've experienced, I've indulged myself in various types of classes and activities.
It didn't mean that I was good at all of them, but I was ready to at least try.
Through web design and coding, I'm able to express myself in a way most accessible to my community.
My hope for the future is to educate those less fortunate. Since I was capable to observe the world, I've learned to have compassion for those around me.
Through The Knowledge Society (TKS), a global innovation program for high schoolers, I hope I can use today's most powerful weapon, technology, to make an impact.
I am currently a sophomore at Marlboro High School, NJ, USA.
Enrolled in the Web Design Pathway Program at my school, I stay active by being on the Advisory Board for the program that organizes events like Fall/Spring Portfolio and meetings with other district teachers/faculty to work on making the program better. Besides Web Design, I like involving myself in math classes (I'm a currently taking PreCalculus).
